Experience values

“Make commerce easy and fun”

👍 Easy

Everyone should be able to turn their idea into an online store without the need for any coding, (e-)commerce or design experience. At the same time, experienced entrepreneurs should be able to run a successful online business with little effort. Every product of MyOnlineStore is designed to be very easy to set-up and maintain, empowering the experienced without overwhelming the inexperienced.

🎉 Fun

Even with the easiest user interface, running an online store can sometimes be a tedious task. To combat this we implement subtle ‘Wow’-moments in our design. Moments that make it feel like Christmas morning. Moments that can break an otherwise monotonous routine. Moments that put a smile on your face. So here’s cake! 🍰
